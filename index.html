About me

I received my B.Sc. from Shandong University in 2020 and my Ph.D. from Peking University in 2025, under the supervision of Shaobo Gan and Disheng Xu. I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at KTH, supervised by Danijela Damjanovic.

Research Interests

Dynamical systems, especially rigidity phenomena in partially hyperbolic and hyperbolic systems. I am particularly interested in the interaction between dynamical systems and geometry, including foliations, complex geometry, conformal geometry, fractal geometry, contact geometry, Lie groups, and group actions.
